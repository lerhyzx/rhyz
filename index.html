Our History

In 2020, Zel, a passionate coffee enthusiast, found herself yearning for a cozy space in her neighborhood where people could gather over a good cup of coffee. Armed with a love for coffee and a dream, she decided to turn an abandoned corner store into what would soon become "Aroma"

Zel sourced high-quality coffee beans from local roasters and she transformed the space with a mix of vintage furniture, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.

The coffee shop's success was a testament to Zel's vision, dedication, and the simple joy of bringing people together over a shared love for exceptional coffee.
